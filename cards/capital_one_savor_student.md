# Capital One — Savor Student Cash Rewards Credit Card

> Contestant dossier. An agent reads this file and argues the case for THIS card.

## Identity
- name: Capital One Savor Student Cash Rewards Credit Card
- codename: The Grocery Gladiator
- issuer: Capital One

## Offer
- annual_fee: $0
- welcome_bonus: $100 cash bonus after spending $300 on purchases within the first 3 months (limited-time offer; VERIFIED active, launched June 9, 2026). NOTE: this bonus appears on the public DIRECT-APPLY page only — Capital One PRE-APPROVAL offer sets show "New Card Member Offer: None" and do NOT carry it. To get the $100, apply via the direct product page, not a pre-approval link.
- intro_apr: None (no 0% intro APR; standard variable APR from day one)
- purchase_apr: 28.99% variable (per account Terms; irrelevant for a pay-in-full player)

## Rewards
- 3% cash back at grocery stores — VERIFIED exclusion (official quote): "Excludes superstores like Walmart® and Target®." Warehouse clubs (Costco, Sam's Club), discount stores, convenience stores, and gas-station groceries also earn only 1%. 3% applies only at standalone supermarkets (Fry's, Safeway, Kroger, Sprouts, Trader Joe's, etc.)
- 3% cash back on dining
- 3% cash back on entertainment (qualified merchant codes only)
- 3% cash back on popular streaming services
- 8% on Capital One Entertainment / 5% on Capital One Travel (hotels, rentals) — irrelevant to this player
- 1% cash back on all other purchases (incl. bills and superstore groceries)
- No foreign transaction fees

## Strengths (argue these)
- Verified, currently-ACTIVE $100 welcome bonus at only $300/3mo — trivially clearable on this player's low spend, and the only top-3 finalist with a real upfront bonus (welcome bonus weighted 32 in rubric). Must apply via direct page to capture it.
- 3% grocery rate (at standalone supermarkets) directly targets the player's largest spend category
- 3% on dining covers the player's secondary spend; the 1% floor applies cleanly to bill payments with no wasted-category penalty
- Unsecured student card — no deposit required, matching the player's hard no-deposit preference (unsecured_card_preference weighted 12)

## Weaknesses (the rival will attack these)
- 3% groceries does NOT apply at Walmart, Target, Costco, Sam's Club, or convenience/discount stores — those earn 1%. If the player buys groceries at superstores, the headline grocery edge collapses to the 1% floor.
- No elevated rate for online shopping — online spend earns only 1%
- $100 bonus is absent on pre-approval offers; a player who applies via a pre-approval link gets the card but NOT the bonus
