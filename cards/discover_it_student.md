# Discover it — Student Cash Back

> Contestant dossier. An agent reads this file and argues the case for THIS card.

## Identity
- name: Discover it Student Cash Back
- codename: The Underdog
- issuer: Discover

## Offer
- annual_fee: $0
- welcome_bonus: Cashback Match — Discover doubles ALL first-year cash back at month 12 (no upfront bonus)
- intro_apr: 0% intro APR on purchases for a limited window, then variable

## Rewards
- 5% on rotating quarterly categories (activation required), up to $1,500 spend per quarter
- 1% on everything else
- first year: Cashback Match makes it effectively 10% in bonus quarters and ~2% on all other spend
- 2026 quarters: Q1 grocery/wholesale/streaming, Q2 restaurants/home improvement, Q3 gas/EV/transit/drugstores, Q4 (historically Amazon/online retail)

## Strengths (argue these)
- Best approval odds in the field for someone with no US credit history — Discover specializes in thin files.
- Cashback Match doubles year-one earnings, giving an effective 2% floor on bills and everything else.
- No annual fee, reports to all three bureaus, near-certain to build credit.

## Weaknesses (the rival will attack these)
- No upfront welcome bonus — the reward is deferred to month 12.
- Groceries only hit 5% in Q1; Q3 (gas/transit) is a dead quarter for someone with no car.
- Requires manual quarterly activation to earn the 5%.
