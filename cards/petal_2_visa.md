# WebBank / Petal — Petal 2 "Cash Back, No Fees" Visa Credit Card

> Contestant dossier. An agent reads this file and argues the case for THIS card.

## Identity
- name: Petal 2 "Cash Back, No Fees" Visa Credit Card
- codename: The Clean Slate
- issuer: WebBank (program managed by Petal)

## Offer
- annual_fee: $0
- welcome_bonus: none
- intro_apr: none

## Rewards
- 1% cash back on all purchases from day one
- 1.25% cash back after 6 consecutive on-time payments
- 1.5% cash back after 12 consecutive on-time payments
- 2%–10% cash back at select merchants via Petal Perks program
- No late fees, no annual fees, no foreign transaction fees

## Strengths (argue these)
- Underwriting uses bank account cash-flow analysis rather than a US credit score — the single strongest approval story for a student with no US credit history (approval_odds_no_us_history weighted 15 in rubric, and a declined application yields zero value from any other card)
- Absolutely no fees of any kind: no annual fee, no late fee, no foreign transaction fee — total cost of ownership is zero regardless of spending behavior
- Rewards grow automatically with responsible behavior, reinforcing builds_credit criterion (weighted 5)
- No category restrictions or rotating activation required — every purchase, including bill payments, earns cash back from the start

## Weaknesses (the rival will attack these)
- No elevated grocery rate — earns only 1% on groceries until 12 months of on-time payments, making it the weakest performer on the player's heaviest spend category (groceries weighted 25)
- No welcome bonus at all — zero upfront value (upfront_welcome_bonus weighted 20 in rubric)
- APR is 28.24%–30.24% variable (as of January 2026) — higher than competing student cards; carrying any balance is costly
